# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 4, 2026

## Introduction

First-class Metronome ("the App") is an open-source Android application. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **on your device only**:

- **Practice sessions**: Start time, end time, and duration of your metronome practice sessions
- **User preferences**: Your settings such as tempo, time signature, and haptic feedback preferences

This data is stored locally in a database on your device and is **never transmitted** to any server or third party.

### Data Not Collected

The App does **not** collect:

- Personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any data transmitted over the internet

## Permissions

The App requests the following permission:

- **Vibrate**: Used to provide haptic feedback when the metronome beats, if enabled in settings

This permission does not access any personal data.

## Data Sharing

The App does **not** share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

- Using the "Clear data" option within the App settings
- Uninstalling the App
- Clearing the App's data through Android system settings

## Open Source

This App is open source. You can review the source code to verify these privacy practices at: [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/adericbourg/FirstClassMetronome)

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/adericbourg/FirstClassMetronome/issues).
