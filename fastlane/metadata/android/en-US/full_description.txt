First-class Metronome is a precise, accessible metronome app designed for musicians who need reliable tempo control during practice.

Features:
- Accurate BPM control with a visual grid of common tempos
- Fine-grained tempo adjustment with configurable increment buttons
- Tap tempo for detecting tempo from live music
- Audio and haptic beat feedback with adjustable vibration strength
- Practice session tracking with a work log
- Material You dynamic colors and dark/light theme support
- Accessibility-first design (WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliant)
- No internet connection required — all data stays on your device
- Free and open source (GPLv3)
